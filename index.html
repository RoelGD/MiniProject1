GSIS TO OFFER LOAN OF UP TO P5M ON APRIL 23 The Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) will open on April 23 the Multi-purpose Loan (MPL) Plus program that offers a credit limit of P5 million. It also extended the maximum payment term to 10 years and relaxed the borrowers’ eligibility requirements.
“We enhanced the MPL program based on the feedback from members in the previous MPL so we can offer a loan program that will best suit their needs. Sa MPL Plus, umaasa ang GSIS na mas marami pa tayong miyembrong matutulungan,” President and General Manager (PGM) Rolando Ledesma Macasaet said.
